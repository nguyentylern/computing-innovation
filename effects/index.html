effects

beneficial effects

Disabled people could more freely access music.

People who may have disabilities and are not able to visit stores to purchase music physically can access music freely from a computer.

harmful effects

Large music artists faced decreased album sales (Mortimer et al., 2012), possibly demotivating them to produce more new music.
